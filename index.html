Game description

The goal of the game is to survive as long as possible. The first phase of the game is the building phase. In this phase you can build structures anywhere on the map. The possible structures you can build are listed below, with their cost and their health. You can build structures from the menu at the bottom right of the map during the build time. When all your structures are gone, you lose.

You can scroll the map above using the arrow keys or by using the mini-map in the top left. After pressing the 'Play' button the game will run for 10 seconds followed by the next build time. Every round a new Portal spawns at a random location and Things become more powerful. Try to kill as many Things and survive as many rounds as possible!

Portal Health: 100

Portals will randomly appear on the map and spawn Things.

Thing Health: 10

Things walk over the map and attack your nearest structures.

Burner Health: 10  Cost: 10

Burners deal damage to anything nearby. However, they will prioritize Things and Portals.

Harvester Health: 10  Cost: 20

Harvesters pull in dead Things and process them for energy. Note that dead Things decay over time!

Beacon Health: 1  Cost: 20

The Things are drawn by Beacons when they are within range. They do not have very much health though!
